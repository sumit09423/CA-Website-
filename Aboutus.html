WHO WE ARE

Over 15 years experience in insurance industry and consultancy

Our identity is forged by the unique blend of our experiences, values, and relationships. Our defining attributes encompass our expertise, commitment to excellence, client-centric approach, and dedication to delivering tailored tax solutions. A A Dubey & Associates, has been set up by the founders & registered under the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India in 2022 with its partners having more than 2 decades of vast experience across industries – insurance, media and manufacturing, and across functions – tax, audit, assurance, financial modelling and budgeting and financial management. We have been serving various corporate houses in their tax compliance & assurance functions through our Head Office in Mumbai and through our associates at various locations. Our organisation stands strong with a team of qualified specialists and professionals driven by a firm belief of sincerity, hard work and continuous learning.
